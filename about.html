About Me

Hello! I'm Pedro Juan Royo, a PhD student in Computational Chemistry at the Day Group in Southampton (UK). I hold a Master of Engineering in Materials Science & Engineering. My research focuses on using the Monte Carlo threshold algorithm to explore the crystal packing energy landscapes of molecular crystals, and see how we can use this information in a crystal structure prediction workflow.

In addition to my research, I have a strong interest in scientific programming and open-source software development. During my time in the Day Group I have contributed to the mol-CSPy code and lead the group meetings to talk about the code development. Some of my other projects are in my GitHub.
